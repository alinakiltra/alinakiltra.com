About · AQUATICO

Exploring the relationship between the body, nature, and graphic expression.

I'm Alina Kiltra — tattoo artist working under the name AQUATICO. My practice is built around custom blackwork and contemporary tattooing that moves with the body rather than sitting on top of it.

Every piece I make starts from a genuine conversation. I'm interested in depth, contrast, and the natural forms that already exist in the places people want to mark — the curve of a shoulder, the line of a forearm, the negative space between.

How it works

01

First contact

Tell me about your idea — the concept, the placement, references that move you. We'll talk through it by message or video call, no rush.

02

Custom design

Every piece is drawn from scratch for your body. I never reuse designs. The image is developed through the lens of graphic expression and natural form.

03

The session

A focused, considered environment. We work at the pace the piece demands — quality over speed, always.

04

Healing & follow-up

I'm available throughout the healing process and for any touch-up the work needs. The piece isn't finished until it's settled into the skin.
